# 🍜 Noodles CSR Survey Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer preferences for Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives using survey data. The goal is to understand which sustainability factors influence customer decisions, willingness to pay, and overall importance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify key CSR initiatives preferred by customers  
- Analyze willingness to pay for sustainability features  
- Evaluate importance of sustainability factors  
- Understand customer behavior and demographics  
- Provide data-driven business recommendations  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)  
- Data Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning Steps
- Removed extra header row  
- Cleaned and standardized column names  
- Handled missing values  
- Converted Likert-scale responses into numerical values  
- Structured dataset for analysis  

---

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed
- CSR Preference Analysis  
- Willingness to Pay Analysis  
- Importance of Sustainability Factors  
- Volunteer Behavior Analysis  
- Income Distribution Analysis  
- Demographic Analysis (CSR by Income)  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Customers strongly prefer sustainability initiatives related to food quality such as local produce and natural ingredients  
- Customers are willing to pay more for high-quality and sustainable food options  
- Sustainability factors like freshness, natural ingredients, and ethical sourcing are highly important  
- Customer engagement in community activities varies across individuals  
- Majority of customers belong to mid-to-high income groups  
- CSR preferences are consistent across all income levels  

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-impact CSR initiatives:
  - Local sourcing  
  - Natural ingredients  
  - Organic products  

- Position sustainability as a premium offering  
- Highlight health and ethical benefits in marketing  
- Educate customers about environmental initiatives  
- Use tiered pricing strategies for different income groups  
- Develop inclusive CSR strategies to engage all customers  

---

## 🎯 Conclusion
The analysis shows that sustainability plays a significant role in customer preferences and decision-making. Customers value CSR initiatives that directly impact food quality and health and are willing to pay for them.

Since sustainability preferences are consistent across income groups, businesses can leverage CSR initiatives as a scalable and strategic advantage.
